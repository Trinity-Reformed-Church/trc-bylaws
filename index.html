Welcome

These are the official bylaws for Trinity Reformed Church. You can always find the latest online version at https://bylaws.trinityreformed.org and download the latest PDF version here. A record of the changes can be found in the Updates section at the end of the book.

Trinity Reformed Church was established on October 27, 1996. These bylaws were last amended on October 24, 2021.

THESE BYLAWS ARE SUBJECT TO ARBITRATION PURSUANT TO THE INDIANA CODE SECTION 34-57-2-1 ET SEQ.
